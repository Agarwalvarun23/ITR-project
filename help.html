Get Help

Welcome to the Medi Guides Help Page! We're here to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have about our services. Below, you’ll find detailed information and answers to common questions to help you navigate our website and make the most of our offerings.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  1. How do I search for a medication?

    To search for a medication, simply type the name of the drug into the search bar located at the top of our homepage. Our database will provide you with detailed information about the medication, including its uses, side effects, dosages, and more.

  2. How do I place an order for medication?

    To place an order:

    • Search for the medication you need.
    • Select the correct dosage and quantity.
    • Click “Add to Cart.”
    • Proceed to checkout by clicking the cart icon and following the prompts to complete your purchase.
  3. Can I track my order?

    Yes, you can track your order. Once your order is processed and shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your order’s status through our website or the courier’s tracking page.

  4. What should I do if I experience side effects from a medication?

    If you experience any side effects, stop taking the medication immediately and consult your healthcare provider. For severe reactions, seek emergency medical attention. You can also report side effects through our website’s contact form for further assistance.

  5. How can I cancel or modify my order?

    To cancel or modify your order, please contact our customer service team as soon as possible. Changes can be made if the order has not yet been shipped. Once shipped, we cannot alter or cancel the order.

  6. What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept various payment methods, including major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and other secure online payment options.

Shipping Information

Domestic Shipping: We offer standard and expedited shipping options within the country. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days, while expedited shipping takes 1-3 business days.

International Shipping: International shipping times vary depending on the destination. Please check our Shipping Policy page for more detailed information.

Returns and Refunds

Return Policy: If you need to return a product, please contact our customer service team within 30 days of purchase. Returned items must be unopened and in their original packaging.

Refund Policy: Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days after receiving the returned item. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used during purchase.
